How Can We Help?

Find answers to common questions about our services, ordering process, and more.

Placing an order is easy! Simply browse our menu, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You can customize your order, add special instructions, and choose your preferred delivery time.

We currently deliver within a 10-mile radius of our kitchen. During checkout, you can enter your address to check if we deliver to your area. We're constantly expanding our delivery zones!

Standard delivery time is 30-45 minutes from when your order is confirmed. During peak hours, it may take up to 60 minutes. You'll receive real-time updates about your order status.

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, PayPal, and cash on delivery. All online payments are processed securely through our encrypted payment gateway.

You can modify or cancel your order within 5 minutes of placing it. After that, please contact our customer service team immediately. Once your order is being prepared, modifications may not be possible.

Yes! We have a wide variety of vegetarian and vegan options clearly marked on our menu. You can filter items by dietary preferences when browsing our menu.

We apologize for any inconvenience! Please contact us immediately at support@cloudkitchen.com or call our customer service line. We'll resolve the issue promptly, either by sending a replacement or providing a full refund.

Yes! We offer a rewards program where you earn points with every order. Sign up for our newsletter to receive exclusive discounts, special offers, and early access to new menu items.

After placing your order, you'll receive a confirmation email with a tracking link. You can also track your order in real-time through your account dashboard or by using the order number provided.
